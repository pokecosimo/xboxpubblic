How to Enable Public Profile on Xbox

Follow these steps to make your Xbox profile public so that others can see your profile details. You can do this from your Xbox console, PC, or mobile phone.

On Xbox Console

  1. Turn on Your Xbox Console
    Make sure your Xbox console is turned on and connected to the internet.
  2. Sign In to Your Xbox Account
    Use your Xbox controller to navigate to the main menu and sign in to your Xbox Live account.
  3. Navigate to Profile Settings
    Press the Xbox button on your controller to open the guide. Go to the System tab and select Settings. Then choose Account and select Privacy & online safety.
  4. Adjust Privacy Settings
    In the Privacy & online safety section, select Xbox Live privacy. Here you will find options to adjust who can see your profile details.
  5. Select "Everyone" for Profile
    Look for the setting related to Profile and choose Everyone. This will make your profile visible to all Xbox users.
  6. Save Your Settings
    After selecting "Everyone," make sure to save your settings. This will update your profile visibility.
  7. Verify Your Profile Visibility
    To confirm that your profile is public, you can check it from another account or ask a friend to view your profile.

On PC

  1. Open the Xbox App
    Launch the Xbox app on your PC. If you don't have it, you can download it from the Microsoft Store.
  2. Sign In to Your Xbox Account
    Sign in with your Xbox Live account credentials.
  3. Access Profile Settings
    Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select Settings from the dropdown menu.
  4. Navigate to Privacy Settings
    Go to the Privacy & online safety section.
  5. Adjust Privacy Settings
    In the Xbox Live privacy section, locate the setting for Profile and set it to Everyone to make your profile public.
  6. Save Changes
    Ensure you save your settings to update your profile visibility.
  7. Verify Your Profile Visibility
    Check your profile from another account or ask a friend to ensure it's set to public.

On Mobile Phone

  1. Open the Xbox App
    Launch the Xbox app on your mobile device. If you don’t have it, download it from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Sign In to Your Xbox Account
    Sign in with your Xbox Live account credentials.
  3. Go to Your Profile
    Tap on your profile icon, usually found in the top-left corner of the screen.
  4. Access Privacy Settings
    Tap on Settings, then navigate to Privacy & online safety.
  5. Adjust Privacy Settings
    In the privacy settings, find the option for Profile and set it to Everyone to make your profile public.
  6. Save Changes
    Save your settings to update your profile visibility.
  7. Verify Your Profile Visibility
    Check your profile from another account or ask a friend to ensure it’s visible to everyone.
